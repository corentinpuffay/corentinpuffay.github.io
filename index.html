Corentin Puffay

Neuroscience & Deep Learning Researcher

I am a researcher fascinated by neuroscience and deep learning. At EPFL, I developed a strong engineering foundation through coursework in programming, machine learning, and neuroscience.

My Ph.D. at KU Leuven focuses on relating continuous speech to brain signals (EEG) using novel deep learning architectures ranging from convolutional networks to transformers. I also leverage recent large language models to extract meaningful language representations to predict brain responses from invasive (electrocorticography) and noninvasive modalities.

The goal is to model speech processing in the brain, develop novel EEG-based diagnostic tools and hearing prostheses, and better understand the mechanisms underlying language processing in the brain.
